Joe Smith is a 28 year old photographer from Manchester, England. He graduated from Manchester University with a degree in photography, and his passion for bird watching goes hand in hand with his photography skills. He currently works at Snaps 4 You.

I've always enjoyed nature and watching how animals operate in their natural environments. Combining this with photography creates beautiful memories that capture the experience. As boring as it may seem to some, I couldn't think of a better way to spend my free time.
